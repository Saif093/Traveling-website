Paris

The city of lights, known for its art, fashion, and cuisine.

Highlights:

  • Louvre Museum
  • Eiffel Tower
  • Montmartre

Explore the Louvre Museum, Eiffel Tower, and experience the charming streets of Montmartre. Don't miss the exquisite French cuisine!

Travel Plans

Here are some suggested travel plans for Paris:

  • Day 1: Visit the Eiffel Tower, explore the Seine River
  • Day 2: Tour the Louvre Museum, stroll through the Tuileries Garden
  • Day 3: Discover Montmartre, visit Sacré-Cœur Basilica
